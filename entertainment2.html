Blvck Entertainment, Rekta Sa Kalye unveil huge summer concert spectacle

By Tempo Desk | February 9, 2024 in Entertainment


Some of the country’s biggest music acts including Rico Blanco, Sandwich, Franco, December Avenue, Al James, Flow G, Omar Baliw, Allison Shore, Nobita, Kiyo, Gins and Melodies, Crazy as Pinoy, Smugglaz, Carm, 6ENSE, Blvck Flowers, Blvck Purple, and many more, are coming together for what is being touted as the biggest event of the year: The Blvck Summer Festival.



Happening on April 13, 9am onwards at the SM Mall of Asia Concert Grounds, the Blvck Summer Festival is made possible by Breakout entertainment company, Blvck Entertainment, in cooperation with Rekta Sa Kalye.

Blvck Entertainment’s big bosses, Engineers Louie and Grace Cristobal are both excited to bring the event to completion.



“Because we are loyal advocates of OPM and we believe in the world-class talents of Filipino artists,” said Grace.

This music festival will be a sun-soaked celebration of local Filipino music and culture.



The event gathers over 30 OPM artists from such diverse genres as Hiphop, P-Pop, Rock.

Tickets are now available for purchase at P3,500 (VIP), P2,200 (Gold), and P1,000 (General Admission) RektaSaKalye.com or the official Shopee and Lazada pages of Rekta Sa Kalye.



Blvck Summer Festival is made possible with the help of Alkaviva Waters Philippines, Grace Electronics Philippines, GLC Infinite Waters International, Blue Green Dragon Marketing, LG Realty Development, Blvck Puffs, and Blvck Creatives Studio.
